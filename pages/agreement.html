Built for real coordination

Agree once. Check forever.

The publication artifact is intentionally compact. It records immutable fingerprints rather than hiding the agreement in an application silo. Your wallet controls signing, while Drops makes the completed record easy to rediscover and verify.

No private keys in Studio: keys and signing material stay with your wallet.
No silent edits: change a term and the displayed hash changes.
No single index required: a compatible verifier can search and recheck the confirmed artifact.
