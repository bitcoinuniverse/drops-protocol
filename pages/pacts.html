The Pacts lifecycle

Start immutable. Stay visible. Verify every transition.

The Pact Seed gives the agreement a stable Bitcoin identity. A live Cell commits its current state. A normal update creates a successor Cell and a compact DPC1 transaction commitment, with a Proof Pack to verify the move.

Seed: fixed agreement identity, ruleset, policy, and initial state commitment.
Cell: ordinary P2TR UTXO with the current state commitment.
Proof: hash-addressed package that verifies the confirmed transition and successor Cell.
