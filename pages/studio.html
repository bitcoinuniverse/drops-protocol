Pacts Studio

Go from a clear idea to a Bitcoin L1 agreement.

Pacts Studio gives people a straightforward way to choose an outcome, set visible terms, review a readable agreement, and publish a compact immutable record through Drops.

No contract language required

Make the important terms visible before anyone signs.

You do not need to learn a contract language to use Studio. It guides people from a clear idea to terms they can read before they publish, including fair-launch terms for an op-drop token such as $DROP, the first token on op-drop.

The Studio promise

Read it in human terms. Preserve it in Bitcoin terms.

Your wallet remains the place where transactions are reviewed, signed, and broadcast. Studio never needs your seed phrase or private keys. It makes the agreement clear enough to review before your wallet commits it.

People and keys: use public addresses and public keys for every visible role.
Terms and limits: check amounts, mint limits, dates, schedules, and allowed actions.
Stable hashes: save or share the fingerprints that prove the reviewed terms are unchanged.
